More Than a Conference

International Conference on Artificial Intelligence Management and Trends (ICAIMT) is a specialized conference on AI, AI trends, and data analytics serves as a vital platform for exploring the latest advancements and understanding how these technologies are reshaping multiple sectors.

ICAIMT brings together industry experts, researchers, and practitioners to discuss applications, trends, and breakthroughs in AI technology and its various applications, ranging from generative AI, machine learning, Open AI to deep learning and natural language processing.
