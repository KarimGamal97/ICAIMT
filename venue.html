Venue

The conference will be held at Abu Dhabi School of Management (ADSM) at the center of Abu Dhabi city. The Faculty of Management and the department of Business Analytics at ADSM will host the conference and provide all logistic support. One of the aims of the Faculty Management is to equip national and international students with up-to-date necessary skills in Data Analysis and AI tools to produce graduates that hold problem-solving skills and technical skills relevant to industry demand. The faculty offers a range of dynamic, technology-driven programs based on international applied curriculum and education principles
