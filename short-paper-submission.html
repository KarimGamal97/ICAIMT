#ICAIMT2025

International Conference on Artificial Intelligence Management and Trends

Conference Date: May 21, 2025

ADSM, Abu Dhabi

Call for Short Papers

The International Conference on Artificial Intelligence Management and Trends (ICAIMT) 2025 invites submissions from researchers including research students, to present their work in artificial intelligence applications and trends.

Deadline April 23,2025

All accepted short papers will be published in the official conference proceedings with an ISBN number. ONLY selected high quality short papers will be invited to submit an extended version for potential publication in the following Impact Factor international academic journal:

  1. Journal of Modelling in management Q2, Emerald
    Special Issue focus: AI for Business and Management
    Guest Editors: Davide La Torre (SKEMA Business School, Université Côte d’Azur, Sophia Antipolis, France, davide.latorre@skema.edu , Dr. Neda Abdelhamid  , (Abu Business Analytics , Dhabi School of Management), Abu Dhabi UAE n.abdelhamid@adsm.ac.ae , Dr. Cinzia Colapinto (Department of Management, University of Venice, Italy.)

 

Submission Guidelines:

Topics: For a comprehensive list of topics, please refer to Conference Tracks

  • Format: Submissions should adhere to the conference's formatting guidelines.
  • Length: While the specific page limit for short papers isn't explicitly mentioned, authors are encouraged to adhere to standard short paper lengths, typically 2–4 pages.
  • Originality: Papers must be original and not under consideration elsewhere.
  • 2400 words maximum including references 
  • Short Paper format: Use the Short Paper template
  • All short papers will be subject to at least 2 peer reviews by experts in the fields.

Short Paper Structure (Suggested Outline)

Section

Content

Title

Clear and Concise Title with author names ,affiliations and email addresses

Abstract

100  words summarizing the aim, method, and key findings

Introduction

Context, research gap, and contribution

Methodology

Research design, tools, data sources (if applicable)

Any Preliminary Results if applicable /

Initial results, insights, or innovative framework/concept

Conclusion

Implications and future work

References

APA format, focus on recent sources

 

The organizers have established these guidelines to ensure a standardized submission format and facilitate the review of contributions for the ICAIMT conference.
