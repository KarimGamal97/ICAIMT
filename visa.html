Visa Information

Once the paper has been accepted, the registered author(s) will be entitled to receive an invitation letter to attend the conference, so they can present their research work. This letter will be sent by the conference local chair to the author who will present the accepted paper(s) at the conference. ICAIMT is not authorized to help authors of accepted papers with the VISA application after issuing the invitation letter. If the visa application is not successful ICAIMT cannot change the decision of the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and will not be engaged in any correspondence with the UAE embassy on behalf of the applicant.

For more information on the visa process: Visa UAE

Zayed International Airport

Dubai International Airport in Abu Dhabi is one of the largest airports in the region. The number of passengers is constantly growing per year. The airports in UAE serve approximately more than 88.2 million passengers per year and connect to 240 different destinations worldwide.

Airport Transportation

Zayed International Airport provides a variety of transportation to the city and vice versa including bus, and taxis. Below are common transportation means to and from Dubai International Airport

Taxi
